About Me

Hi, I'm Abhishek, an NFT artist from India. My art is a reflection of my love for traditional hand-drawn art and my fascination with technology.

My style is a fusion of traditional and digital techniques, where I create hand-drawn art and then enhance it using digital tools to create stunning, unique pieces that showcase my artistic vision in my NFT. I'm particularly inspired by Indian culture and mythology, and my artworks often feature Indian gods and goddesses, as well as scenes from Indian epics like the Ramayana and Mahabharata.

Through my art, I aim to showcase the rich history and heritage of India, while also exploring new ways of representing traditional art forms in a digital medium. Each of my NFTs is a one-of-a-kind masterpiece that blends intricate details and bold colors, creating a captivating visual experience for collectors and enthusiasts. The world of NFT art has given me an opportunity to share my work with a global audience and connect with other artists and collectors from around the world. I'm proud to be a part of the vibrant and growing NFT art community, and I'm excited to see where this journey takes me next.
